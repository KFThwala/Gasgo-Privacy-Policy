Gasgo Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gasgo! This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our gas delivery services through our mobile app or website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email and delivery address when placing an order.
  • Payment Information: Payment details (e.g., M-Pesa, card info) processed securely by third-party providers. We do not store your payment credentials.
  • Location Data: If enabled, we use GPS data to locate your address for deliveries.
  • Usage Data: Basic app analytics such as device type and app interactions to improve service.

3. How We Use Information

  • To process and deliver your gas orders.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To improve our app and user experience.
  • To send delivery updates, receipts, and service-related notifications.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share certain information with:

  • Delivery partners to fulfill your order.
  • Payment processors (e.g., M-Pesa or bank APIs) for transactions.
  • Government authorities when required by law.

5. Location Data

Your location is used only to determine delivery locations. You can disable location permissions at any time in your device settings, but this may limit delivery functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@mocapital.co.mz
